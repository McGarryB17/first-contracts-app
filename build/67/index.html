AMERICAN ORDNANCE LLC, Milan, Tenn., has been awarded a $7,547,742 cost-plus-fixed-fee contract for calendar year 2014 care, maintenance and surveillance of field service stocks and packaging, crating and handling of field service stocks at Milan Army Ammunition Plant. Funding is from fiscal year 2014 operations and maintenance Army appropriations. Work is to be performed at Milan, Tenn., with an estimated completion date of Dec. 31, 2014. One bid was solicited with one received. The U.S. Army Contracting Command's Rock Island Arsenal, Rock Island, Ill., is the contracting activity (W52P1J-09-G-0001).
